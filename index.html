Rules

Be respectful of differences: Absolutely no indecent 18+ or offensive talk/puns. No hateful, disrespectful, messages directed towards people's differences. This includes but is not limited to any and all aspects of technology, religious or political beliefs, hobbies, physical or mental disabilities.

No illegal activities: Scamming, Social Engineering, Doxxing, Warez, Copyright Infringement, Piracy Trading etc.

No Unsolicited DMs: Ask for permission from the user in the channel before sending direct messages.

No Unapproved Advertisement: Do not self-promote or promote commercial services unless approved. Not even in DMs.

Keep everything “Safe For Work”: This means absolutely no F words, no swearing and no material of strong political or religious character. If in doubt, do not post it. This applies to your Display Picture, Status, and Messages.

Check these at the door: Ego, Hate, Troll, and everything negative.

Staff have the final say: Discord staff has the final say on penalties for rule violations.
